Welcome to Neddar

The game takes place in the Neddar region. In this region they have a tradion, the anual Gym Challenge, where trainers embark on a journey to collect all 8 gym badges. In what order they do, that's totally up to them.

The pokemon found in the Neddar region are all pokemon never seen before!

Notible mechanics

In this game there are some fun mechanics that are worth mentioning:

  • 2 new typings added: Light & Sound
  • You can battle the gym leaders in any order
  • Difficulty settings: Easy, Normal, Hard
  • Build-in randomizer to randomize your game (includes pokemon from gen 1 to 8)
  • Easy access to rare candies (for when doing challenge runs for example)
