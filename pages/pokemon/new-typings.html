Typings

In this game we have introduces 2 new typings: Sound type and Light type. Both have a mixture of existing moves that are now a new type and some newly created moves.

Sound type

  • Sound weaknesses are: Light, Psychic
  • Sound resistances are: Dragon, Ghost, Sound
  • Sound is super effective against: Water, Fairy
  • Sound is not very effective against: Dark, Bug, Sound

Light type

  • Light weaknesses are: Water, Electric
  • Light resistances are: Dark, Fairy, Light
  • Light is super effective against: Dark, Sound
  • Light is not very effective against: Ghost, Light
