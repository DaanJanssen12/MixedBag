Homebrew Content

Homebrew Content is all material which is not released by Wizards of the Coast. The content you'll find here is the content that we include when for example you check the 'include homebrew content' box at the Character Generator. All content below will link to the original page, and please note that the content listed down below is made by various authors. If the piece of Homebrew is not made by us, you will see the authors name being included.
